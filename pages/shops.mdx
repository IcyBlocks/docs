Players have the ability to rob other stores to steal the owner's money. <br/>

## Features

> **Robbery System:** This resource includes a robbery system within the player owned shops <br />
> **Configuration:** Everything is changeable! <br />
> **Locales:** Translate-able to all languages <br />
> **Compatibility:** Compatible with all frameworks and all dispatch exports are changeable <br />
> **Store-Based Robbery Cooldown:** Server-sided cooldown system that applies to all players, based on each shop <br />
> **Optimization:** This resource runs on 0ms to 1ms on idle and while in a shop zone. <br />
